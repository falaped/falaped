<objective>
Deliver the end-to-end happy path for PHOTO-01 + PHOTO-02: a doctor picks a photo in the patient form, it is compressed in the browser, uploaded to the private bucket via a gated Server Action that captures consent, and the photo then shows on all three avatar surfaces (hero, list thumbnail, case header) through a server-resolved short-lived signed URL. After this slice a real user can attach a photo and see it everywhere.

Purpose: This is the core capability of the phase. It turns the foundation from Plan 01 into a working feature — the doctor's first observable win.
Output: The compression util, four patient photo modules (upload/update/signed-url singular + batch), the upload action, the form photo field + consent checkbox, and `<AvatarImage>` on the three surfaces with server-side URL resolution. The two Plan-01 Wave-0 specs turn GREEN.

Decisions implemented in this plan: D-02 (store path, sign at render), D-03 (profile_id scope), D-04 (blocking consent checkbox), D-05 (write consent_given + consent_at), D-06 (consent re-required on replace), D-07 (classic file input, no capture), D-08 (single replaceable photo via upsert), D-09 (client-side compression — Free plan, no native transforms), D-10 (photo on three surfaces: hero, list, case header), D-11 (circular avatar with initials fallback), D-12 (photo SHOWS in the list/table — conscious divergence from Phase 1's no-age-in-lists rule).
</objective>
